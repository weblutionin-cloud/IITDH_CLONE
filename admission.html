admission at IIT Dharwad

IIT Dharwad offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. The institute selects students based on merit and national entrance examinations.

Undergraduate Programs

B.Tech admission are through JEE Advanced. Eligible students must fulfill the eligibility criteria as per IIT standards.

Postgraduate Programs

M.Tech and Ph.D. admission are conducted through GATE and institute-specific selection processes.

Application Procedure

Applicants can apply online via the official IIT Dharwad admission portal. Ensure all documents are submitted before the deadline.
