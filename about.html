About IIT Dharwad

Indian Institute of Technology Dharwad (IIT Dharwad) was established in 2016 and is one of the premier institutes of technology in India. The institute aims to provide world-class education, research, and innovation in engineering and technology.

The campus is located at Chikkamalligawad, Dharwad, Karnataka, and houses state-of-the-art laboratories, modern classrooms, and residential facilities for students and staff. IIT Dharwad is committed to academic excellence, fostering innovation, and contributing to the societal development of India.
